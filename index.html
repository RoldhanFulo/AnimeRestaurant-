read our story

we've been making delicious food since 1999

Yukihira's resto bar began on December 6, 1999 in the city of Okinawa, Japan. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch. Yukihira's resto bar has three generations of family member working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication, Yukihira's resto bar began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional Japanese food.
